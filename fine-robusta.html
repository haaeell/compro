Agro Surya Berdiri

Fine Robusta

  • Defect: <2
  • Origin: Temanggung, Central Java, Indonesia
  • Process: Natural / Dry Process
  • Moisture: 12% – 13.5%
  • Altitude: 900 – 1200 masl
  • Tasty: Bold, low acid, floral

Fine Robusta beans represent the top quality from Temanggung. With less than 2% defect rate, this coffee delivers a clean cup profile, bold body, and smooth floral notes — perfect for premium espresso blends.
