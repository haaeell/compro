Agro Surya Berdiri

Robusta Grade 2

  • Defect: 8.3% – 10%
  • Origin: Temanggung, Central Java, Indonesia
  • Process: Natural / Dry Process
  • Moisture: 12% – 13.5%
  • Altitude: 900 – 1200 masl
  • Tasty: Bold, low acid, floral

Robusta Grade 2 provides an excellent balance between flavor and value. This coffee features a slightly higher defect rate but maintains strong body and clean notes, ideal for mass roasting and instant blends.
