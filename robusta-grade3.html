Agro Surya Berdiri

Robusta Grade 3

  • Defect: 14.6% – 20%
  • Origin: Temanggung, Central Java, Indonesia
  • Process: Natural / Dry Process
  • Moisture: 12% – 13.5%
  • Altitude: 900 – 1200 masl
  • Tasty: Bold, low acid, floral

Robusta Grade 3 is suitable for large-scale production and instant coffee base. Despite higher defect percentage, it retains Temanggung’s signature strong body and earthy notes.
