Robusta Grade 1

  • Defect: 3.3%
  • Origin: Temanggung, Central Java, Indonesia
  • Process: Natural / Dry Process
  • Moisture: 12% – 13.5%
  • Altitude: 900 – 1200 masl
  • Tasty: Bold, low acid, floral

Robusta Grade 1 offers high consistency and balanced flavor. With low defect rate and a deep aroma, it’s widely used for export blends and provides a bold character with soft acidity.
