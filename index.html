PT AGRO SURYA BERDIRI

Making the Archipelago Fragrant, Bringing Natural Wealth to the World. We are supplier and exporter of premium Indonesian coffee, from farmer's hands to coffee experts worldwide.

Our History

Driven by a passion and commitment to enhancing the lives of local farmers, this company was founded to become a trusted supplier of Robusta coffee for both local and global markets. Every step of the production process is carried out with great care to ensure that each coffee bean delivers a unique and distinctive taste and aroma. With a strong dedication to farmer empowerment and producing premium-quality Robusta coffee, Agro Surya Berdiri is poised to export its products worldwide. The company firmly believes that through collaboration and dedication, it can contribute to a sustainable and high-quality global coffee industry.

Step 1: Farmer Collaboration

Partnering with local farmers to ensure fair trade and quality beans from the start.

Step 2: Harvest & Selection

Carefully handpicking the ripest Robusta cherries to maintain natural richness.

Step 3: Processing & Roasting

Processed and roasted with precision to highlight Indonesia’s bold coffee flavor.

Step 4: Export Worldwide

Delivering premium Robusta coffee to global markets with pride and dedication.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a supplier of green robusta coffee beans and its finished products, which can provide the best value and quality to our customers.

Our mission is to network with Indonesian natural product players and local farmers, ensuring the best quality is always maintained.
